### EEA Context

Norway is not an EU member state but participates in the European Economic Area (EEA). Many EU financial regulations (MiFID II, Solvency II, CRD/CRR, PSD2, DORA, etc.) are incorporated into Norwegian law through the EEA Agreement. This Tool covers the Norwegian implementation (forskrifter, rundskriv, veiledninger) but does NOT cover the underlying EU legislation directly. For EU regulations, see the [EU Regulations MCP](https://github.com/Ansvar-Systems/EU_compliance_MCP).

## Currency and Staleness

- Database updates are **manual** and may lag official publications by weeks or months
- Norwegian financial regulation changes through new forskrifter, amendments, rundskriv, and supervisory decisions
- A regulation marked "in force" may have been amended or repealed since the last database update
- This Tool does NOT automatically track amendments

## Coverage Gaps

This Tool does **NOT** include:

1. **Full legal text** -- contains representative summaries only
2. **Finansklagenemnda decisions** -- Norwegian Financial Complaints Board rulings are not covered
3. **EU financial directives** -- covered separately by the EU Regulations MCP
4. **Stortinget proceedings** -- parliamentary debates on financial legislation are not included
5. **Individual firm supervision reports** -- confidential supervisory correspondence is not covered
6. **Historical regulation versions** -- limited to current in-force versions
